Typography

Our typography says as much about our brand as our colors, symbol, or logo. We have two main typefaces: Claremont and Boing. Both have been chosen with the same principles as the rest of the brand – being approachable and reliable.

Headlines

Claremont is a serif typeface with large serifs that make it stable, with a presence. Only use this for headlines or instances for large type.

Body copy

Boing is a friendly sans-serif with circular forms that embody Shipt's character. Use Boing for any instances of body copy and captions. Though it has six weights, our go-to's are Boing Light, Medium, and Bold.
